About

Data-driven businesses often struggle to translate raw numbers into clear business direction, which results in missed growth opportunities and slow decision-making. I work as a Data Analyst / Business Analyst, helping organizations make confident decisions through structured sales analysis, KPI tracking, and financial reporting using SQL, Power BI, Excel, and Python.

In my retail sales project, I analyzed Year-over-Year performance to identify product-level growth patterns. Mountain-100 Silver (44) showed continuous growth -reaching ₹1,420,000 in 2013 with positive variance across both years. In contrast, Road-150 Red (62) demonstrated consistent decline, enabling clear product performance classification (📈 growth vs 📉 decrease).

I built dashboards tracking Total Sales, Recency, and Average Monthly Spend, and performed customer segmentation (VIP, Regular, New; age-based clusters below 20 to 50+). This helped uncover behavioral insights and revenue contribution patterns.

My approach combines storytelling, clean dashboard design, and business thinking because I believe: “Data should simplify decisions, not complicate them.”

I am currently open to full-time roles and internships in Data Analytics or Business Analysis. Best way to connect: LinkedIn DM, email, or direct call.
